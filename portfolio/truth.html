Project Overview

The Truth or Dare App is an interactive platform designed to encourage users to disconnect from technology and reconnect with nature through playful truth-or-dare challenges. The app was developed using a user-centered design approach, incorporating tools like Figma for prototyping, as well as user flow diagrams, surveys, and personas to create a seamless and engaging user experience. The primary goal of the app is to inspire users to step away from screens and engage with nature.

Learning Takeaways

  • Understanding and applying user design flow diagrams, personas, and connecting them to our interviews
  • Developing proficiency in design platforms such as Figma
  • Learning how to analyze and interpret user research to meet user needs and expectations
